  • Legal Issues: Using a modded app violates PicsArt’s terms of service and copyright policies, which could lead to account suspension or legal action.
  • Lack of Updates: Modded apps often lack regular updates, which can affect performance and introduce compatibility issues.
  • Ethical Implications: Using unpaid versions of premium software deprives developers of fair compensation for their work.
